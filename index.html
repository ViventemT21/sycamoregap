Clue 6

Hola Amigo! Yes, I’m originally from Mexico. I belong to the Crassulaceae family. At first glance, I may appear as a mushroom. Overlapping, rounded triangles of one of my body parts form a Fibonacci spiral. I do not contain chlorophyll. Although I have a waxy whitish colour, I can tend to be blue-grey when I grow in partial shade; in full, hot sun, grey-pink. I absorb nutrients through a unique process called myco-heterotrophy. Who am I?
